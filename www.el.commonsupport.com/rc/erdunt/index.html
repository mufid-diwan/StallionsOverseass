Metal recycling industry

The metal recycling industry encompasses a wide range of metals. The more frequently recycled metals are scrap steel, iron (ISS), lead, aluminum, copper, stainless steel, and zinc

Common non-ferrous metals are copper, brass, aluminum, zinc, magnesium, tin, nickel, and lead.

Exotic metals contain rare elements such as cobalt, mercury, titanium, tungsten, arsenic, beryllium, bismuth, cerium, cadmium, niobium, indium, gallium, germanium, lithium, selenium, tantalum, tellurium, vanadium, and zirconium.

What are the different categories of Oil found worldwide?

There are around 161 different types of oil found around the world. The various forms of crude oil found throughout the world are categorised as Brent, Dubai Crude, West Texas Intermediate, and so on. The sulphur content of the products is used to classify them.

Who analyses and does research of the Oil and Natural gas supply?

NETL conducts foundational research to improve the production, processing, transportation, and storage of U.S. oil and natural gas resources.
